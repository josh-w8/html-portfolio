The Best Video Games According to Josh

My top 3 video games of all-time.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Utterly brilliant. The expansive open world and attention to detail are second to none.

Grand Theft Auto V

As above but a slightly older game and I prefer the Western style genre.

Skyrim

Beautiful world design with a great storyline and even better lore. I love the Elder Scrolls franchise and this one tops the lot.
